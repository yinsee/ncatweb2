Features

Community Built

Community driven & fair launch. Our developer has burned all the team tokens and renounced contract ownership to provide security for the whole community!

LP Grinder

5% of every transaction contributes toward automatically generating further liquidity for the NCAT Community. Benefiting long term holders the most!

Frictionless Yield

4% of every transaction is taken and re distributed to all NCAT holders. The burn address is also a holder thus each transaction helps deflate the supply.

Highly Secured

All initial liquidity provided has been locked with UniCrypt. The contract owner has been renounced, leaving the contract trustless for the NCAT community.

DAO

What community token would be complete without a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO)? Propose & Vote on creative ideas brought forward by the community! Allows the community to get a picture on which direction the majority of users want to head and helps steer the project's direction!
